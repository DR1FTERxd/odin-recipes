šaltibarščiai

Šaltibarščiai- cold summer soup based on beets and milk.

Ingredients

For the Soup

  • 200g or 7 oz boiled & cooled beetroot (about 2 medium beets)
  • 100g or 3.5 oz cucumber (about 2 large pickling cucumbers, either fresh or pickled see note above)
  • 6 spring onions or 10 green onion leaves
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1.5 litres or 3 pints kefir (or 500 ml or 1 pint thick natural yoghurt and 1 litre or 2 pints buttermilk)
  • Bunch fresh dill
  • Juice of ½ a lemon (optional)
  • Salt to taste

For the Side Dish

  • 200 g or 7 oz potatoes
