Cepelinai

Cepelinai or didzkukuliai are potato dumplings. It has been described as a national dish of Lithuania

Ingredients

For the Meat Filling

  • 1 pound ground pork, or an equal mix of pork, beef, and veal
  • 1 medium onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg

For the Dumplings

  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 8 large patatoes, peeled and finely grated, about 5 pounds
  • 2 large Idaho or Russet potatoes, about 1 1/2 pounds, peeled, boiled, and riced
  • 1 medium onion, finely grated
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt, more as needed
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

For the Gravy

  • 1/2 pound bacon, diced
  • 1 large onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons milk, as needed
